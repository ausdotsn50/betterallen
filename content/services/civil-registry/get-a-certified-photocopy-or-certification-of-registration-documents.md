# Get a Certified Photocopy or Certification of Registration Documents — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Civil Registry Office issues certified photocopies and certifications of any civil registration document on file with the office — including birth, death, and marriage certificates. These are accepted as official copies for legal, personal, and government transactions.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                                                  |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Civil Registry Office                                                          |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                                       |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                                                    |
| **Who May Avail**             | Document owner, mother, father, immediate family, guardian, or authorized representative |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Approximately 10 minutes                                                                 |
| **Fees**                      | ₱50.00 per document; ₱100.00 for Certification of Status                                 |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Proceed to the Civil Registry Office and present your requirements

- Visit the Municipal Civil Registry Office and present your complete requirements.
- Staff receives, evaluates, and verifies the documents to be certified (approximately 2 minutes).

### Step 2: Pay at the Municipal Treasurer's Office

- Proceed to the MTO and pay the applicable fee.
- An Official Receipt will be issued (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 3: Return to the Civil Registry and receive your certified document

- Return and present your Official Receipt.
- Staff prepares and certifies the civil registry document (approximately 3 minutes).
- The Municipal Civil Registrar affixes their signature.
- The certified true copy is released to you.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID _(for the document owner, parents, or immediate family)_
- [ ] Authorization Letter or Special Power of Attorney (SPA) and Valid ID _(for authorized representatives)_

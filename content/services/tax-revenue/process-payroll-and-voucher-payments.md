# Process Payroll and Voucher Payments — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Treasurer's Office disburses funds for payrolls and payment vouchers for government employees and suppliers. Vouchers must have complete documentation and required signatures before disbursement can be processed.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                            |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Treasurer's Office                                       |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                              |
| **Who May Avail**             | Government employees, suppliers, and payees with approved vouchers |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 31 minutes                                                         |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                               |

---

## How to Process

### Step 1: Submit your complete payment voucher

- Ensure your voucher has all required signatures from concerned offices.
- The Cashier or Clerk checks that the voucher is complete and ready for payment (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 2: Check issuance and signing

- A check is issued and signed by the Municipal Treasurer and the Mayor (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 3: Return from the Mayor's Office with Official Receipt

- Upon return from the Mayor's Office, the MTO checks the Official Receipt issued by the payee and records it in the cashbook (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 4: Obligation by the Budget Office

- The Budget Office obligates the payroll, which is signed by the department heads and the Local Chief Executive (approximately 15 minutes).

### Step 5: Preparation of withdrawal vouchers and payment

- Withdrawal vouchers and checks are prepared, signed by the Treasurer and LCE, and the payroll is paid out (approximately 5 minutes).

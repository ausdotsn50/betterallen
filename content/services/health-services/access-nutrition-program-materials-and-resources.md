# Access Nutrition Program Materials and Resources — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Nutrition Action Office (MNAO) distributes nutrition program-related materials to community members, caregivers, and nutrition workers. These include nutritional status reports, IEC materials (flyers, comics), Pabasa sa Nutrisyon handbooks, micronutrient supplements, and other resources that support good nutrition practices.

## How to Avail

### Step 1: Inquire about available materials at the MNAO

- Visit the MNAO and inquire about available nutrition materials, or receive materials as part of a counseling or program session.
- Staff checks availability of the requested materials (approximately 5 minutes).
- If the materials are not currently available, the MNAO Designate will discuss the situation with you and agree on a schedule for when you can receive them (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Receive materials and sign the acknowledgment receipt

- If materials are available, they are released to you.
- Staff provides a brief description of the materials issued and checks completeness of entries in the acknowledgment receipt or logbook (approximately 5 minutes).
- Sign the acknowledgment receipt or logbook to confirm receipt.

---

## Requirements

- **For individual pieces**: No requirements needed — walk in directly.
- **For bulk quantities**: Submit a Letter of Request addressed to the MNAO.

### Eligibility

- Malnourished children aged 0–59 months
- Pregnant and lactating women
- School children with nutritional concerns
- Barangay Nutrition Scholars and community nutrition workers

---

## Types of Materials Available

- IEC materials (flyers, comics, posters on nutrition)
- Pabasa sa Nutrisyon handbooks for nutrition workers
- Municipal Nutritional Status reports
- Micronutrient supplements _(subject to availability)_
- Other nutrition program-related materials

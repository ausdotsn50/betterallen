# Get Free Vegetable Seeds for Backyard Gardening — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) distributes free vegetable seeds to residents of Allen who want to start or maintain a backyard garden for household food production. Seeds are available while supplies last.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                            |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen              |
| **Who May Avail**             | All residents of Allen             |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 5 minutes                          |
| **Fees**                      | None                               |

---

## How to Avail

### Step 1: Go to the MAO and request your preferred vegetable seeds

- Visit the MAO and tell the staff which vegetable seeds you would like.
- Staff will interview you, have you sign the masterlist and client action sheet, and release your chosen seeds (approximately 5 minutes).

---

## Requirements

No documentary requirements needed. Simply visit the MAO and make your request.

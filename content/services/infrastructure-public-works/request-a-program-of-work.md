# Request a Program of Work — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Engineering Office prepares and issues Programs of Work (POW) for proposed barangay infrastructure projects. The POW includes the Detailed Engineering Design (DED) and cost estimates required before a project can proceed to funding approval.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                 |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Engineering Office (MEO)                      |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                      |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                   |
| **Who May Avail**             | Barangay officials and their authorized representatives |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Approximately 81 hours and 5 minutes (about 2 weeks)    |
| **Fees**                      | None stated                                             |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Request a Program of Work at the MEO

- The Barangay Official or authorized representative visits the MEO and formally requests a Program of Work for the proposed barangay project.
- MEO staff gathers all relevant data about the project (approximately 30 minutes).

### Step 2: Wait approximately 2 weeks for preparation

- MEO staff prepares the Detailed Engineering Design (DED) and Program of Work (POW), which is reviewed and verified by the Municipal Engineer for approval (approximately 80 hours).
- Follow up with the MEO after 2 weeks.

### Step 3: Review the Program of Work

- The Barangay Official or representative receives and reviews the prepared POW.
- MEO staff asks for further comments or revisions on the project (approximately 30 minutes).

### Step 4: Proceed to the grantor for fund approval

- The Barangay Official or representative proceeds to the grantor (funding agency or office) for approval and confirmation of fund availability.
- MEO receives a copy of the approved POW once granted (approximately 5 minutes).

---

## Requirements

No prior documentary requirements needed. The Barangay Official or representative simply visits the MEO and presents the details of the proposed project.

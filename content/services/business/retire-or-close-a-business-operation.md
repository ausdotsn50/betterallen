# Retire or Close a Business Operation — Municipality of Allen

If your business has closed or stopped operations, you must file an application for business retirement or closure. This updates the Municipal Government's records and prevents the accumulation of unpaid taxes and penalties.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                            |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Business Permits and Licensing Section Office      |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Business                             |
| **Who May Avail**             | Business owners filing for retirement or closure   |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 1 day (with complete requirements)                 |
| **Fees**                      | Any outstanding taxes and fees due (if applicable) |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your application letter and requirements

- Write an application letter notifying the BPLS of your intent to close or retire your business. Include the reason and date of retirement.
- Submit together with all required documents.
- BPLS staff will assess any outstanding taxes and fees due.

### Step 2: Receive your Order of Payment

- BPLS prepares and signs your Order of Payment.
- You will receive the signed Order of Payment and your application letter.
- Proceed to the Municipal Treasurer's Office (MTO) to settle any outstanding amounts.

### Step 3: Pay outstanding taxes and proceed to BPLS

- Pay any taxes and fees due at the MTO cashier and receive your Official Receipt.
- Return to the BPLS and present your Official Receipt.

### Step 4: Claim your Certificate of Closure

- BPLS staff prepares your Certificate of Closure.
- The document is forwarded to the Municipal Treasurer for signature.
- Claim your Certificate of Closure from the BPLS.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Application letter stating the reason and date of business retirement or closure
- [ ] Sworn Statement of Gross Receipts (from the last renewal period up to the intended closure date)
- [ ] Closure Certificate from the Barangay — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] Original copy of your Business Permit
- [ ] Official Receipts issued by the Municipal Treasurer's Office
- [ ] Sales Book
- [ ] Map or sketch of business location
- [ ] Board Resolution regarding closure _(for corporations and cooperatives only)_

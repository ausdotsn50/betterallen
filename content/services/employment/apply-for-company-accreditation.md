# Apply for Company Accreditation — Municipality of Allen

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) accredits local and overseas companies that wish to partner with PESO for job facilitation services such as job fairs and local or special recruitment activities. Accreditation ensures the legitimacy of the company's documents and its compliance with DOLE and POEA requirements.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Approach the PESO Information Desk

- Visit the PESO Office and inquire about company accreditation.
- Staff will refer you to the assigned focal person (approximately 2 minutes).

### Step 2: Submit your documents for evaluation

- Present and submit all required documents.
- Staff receives, checks completeness, validates, and endorses documents to the PESO Manager (approximately 15 minutes).
- Validation with DOLE or POEA may be conducted if needed.

### Step 3: PESO Manager review and approval

- Proceed to the PESO Manager for approval.
- The Manager validates documents, approves the accreditation, and coordinates with the company for the issuance of the Certificate of Accreditation.

## Requirements

- [ ] 1 copy of Letter of Intent — _Company_
- [ ] 1 copy of Company Profile — _Company_
- [ ] 1 copy of Latest Business Permit — _Company_
- [ ] 1 copy of DOLE Registry of Establishment — _DOLE_

- [ ] 1 copy of SEC or DTI Registration Certificate — _SEC or DTI_

### For Overseas/Private Recruitment Companies

- [ ] 1 copy of Letter of Intent — _Company_
- [ ] 1 copy of Company Profile — _Company_
- [ ] 1 copy of POEA License — _POEA_
- [ ] 1 copy of approved Manpower Job Order from POEA — _POEA_
- [ ] 1 copy of Certificate of No Pending Cases and DOLE Registration — _DOLE_
- [ ] 1 copy of SEC/DTI Registration Certificate and BIR Certificate — _SEC/DTI/BIR_
- [ ] 1 copy of updated list of job vacancies with qualifications — _Company_

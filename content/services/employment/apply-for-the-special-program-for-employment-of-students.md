# Apply for the Special Program for Employment of Students — Municipality of Allen

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) facilitates enrollment in the Special Program for Employment of Students (SPES) under Republic Act No. 7323, as amended by RA 9547. SPES provides temporary employment to disadvantaged youth during summer, Christmas vacation, or any time of the year to supplement family income and help ensure beneficiaries can continue their education.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Approach the PESO Information Desk

- Visit the PESO Office and inquire about the SPES program.
- Staff will refer you to the assigned focal person (approximately 2 minutes).

### Step 2: Submit your requirements and undergo initial screening

- Present and submit all required documents to the PESO staff.
- Staff receives, checks completeness, evaluates, and conducts initial screening of your application (approximately 15 minutes).
- If qualified, staff provides the SPES form and assists you in accomplishing it.

### Step 3: Submit the accomplished SPES form

- Submit your completed SPES form.
- Staff schedules your SPES orientation (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 4: Attend the SPES orientation

- Attend the mandatory orientation covering program rules, do's and don'ts, payroll procedures, and total number of working days (approximately 1 hour).
- PESO staff facilitates deployment to your assigned office or work area.

### Step 5: Complete your work assignment

- Report to your assigned office or area and complete the required working days.
- PESO staff processes your payroll upon completion.

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] 1 original copy of SPES Application Form — _PESO Office_
- [ ] 1 original copy of Parent's Consent with guardian contact information _(for minors)_ — _Applicant_
- [ ] 1 photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate — _Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA)_
- [ ] 1 copy of Parent's Certificate of Indigency — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] 1 original copy of Barangay Clearance — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] 1 photocopy of proof of school enrollment (class card, Form 138, or enrollment form) — _School_
- [ ] 1 copy of Out-of-School Youth (OSY) certification _(for OSY applicants)_ — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] 2 passport-size ID pictures with white background — _Computer shop_

### Eligibility

- Students or out-of-school youth from indigent families
- At least 15 but not more than 30 years old

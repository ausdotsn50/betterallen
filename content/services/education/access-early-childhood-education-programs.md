# Access Early Childhood Education Programs — Municipality of Allen

The MSWDO, through its Child Development Workers (CDWs), implements Early Childhood Care and Development (ECCD) programs for children aged 2 to 4 years old. Day care sessions are held in barangay day care centers across Allen.

---

| **Estimated Processing Time** | Enrollment period: approximately 6 months; sessions are held regularly throughout the school year |
| **Fees**                      | None (Moving-up ceremony contribution: ₱450.00)                                                   |

---

## How to Enroll

### Step 1: Look out for barangay mapping and listing

- The Child Development Worker (CDW) conducts a survey and master listing of children aged 2–5 years old in your barangay (approximately 1 week).

### Step 2: Register your child during enrollment

- Approach your barangay CDW to register your child.
- Accomplish the required intake and registration forms.
- Attend the Bayanihan Bulilit and Parents Orientation session.

### Step 3: Attend day care sessions

- Your child attends regular day care sessions (approximately 40 minutes per session per child).
- The ECCD Checklist is conducted every semester (every 3 months for children under monitoring).
- Parents and guardians participate in monthly meetings with the CDW (approximately 2–3 hours).

### Step 4: Moving-up ceremony

- At the end of the school year, children who complete the program participate in a moving-up ceremony (approximately 3–4 hours).
- A contribution of ₱450.00 is collected for this event.

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Accomplished intake/registration form — _Child Development Worker (CDW)_
- [ ] Birth Certificate — _Local Civil Registry or PSA_
- [ ] ECCD Checklist _(accomplished by CDW during enrollment)_ — _CDW_

### Eligibility

- Children aged 2 to 4 years old residing in Allen.

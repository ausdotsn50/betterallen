# Submit an Ordinance or Resolution for Approval — Municipality of Allen

The Sangguniang Bayan (SB) Office receives and processes proposed ordinances and resolutions necessary for an efficient and effective municipal government. Submissions are reviewed by the relevant SB committee before being brought to plenary session for approval and transmittal to the Local Chief Executive (LCE) for final signing.

## How to Submit

### Step 1: Sign in and submit your documents to the SB Secretariat

- Sign in at the SB Office client logbook and submit all required documents to the SB Secretariat.
- Staff checks completeness of the submitted documents (approximately 20 minutes).
- If incomplete, documents are returned with advice to complete all requirements.
- If complete, documents are received with the date, time, and signature of the receiving personnel.

### Step 2: Wait for inclusion in the Calendar of Business

- The SB Secretary includes the matter in the Calendar of Business (1 Session Day).
- The matter is referred to the concerned SB committee.

### Step 3: Attend the Committee Meeting or Hearing

- The concerned committee (e.g., Committee on Barangay Affairs, Committee on Finance, Budget and Appropriation) conducts a committee meeting or hearing (approximately 2 hours).
- The committee renders a committee report (1 Session Day) and the measure is included for 2nd reading (1 Session Day), then 3rd and final reading.

### Step 4: Preparation and signing of the resolution or ordinance

- The SB Secretary prepares copies of the resolution for signature of the Presiding Officer and SB Members (approximately 30 minutes to 1 hour).
- Final copies are signed by all signatories (2–3 days, depending on availability).

### Step 5: Transmittal to the LCE for approval

- The SB Secretary transmits the resolution or ordinance to the Local Chief Executive (Municipal Mayor) for signing and approval (approximately 10 minutes for transmittal; maximum of 3 days for LCE signing).
- Upon receipt from the Mayor's Office, the SB staff records the approved document and informs you (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 6: Claim your copy of the approved resolution or ordinance

- Return to the SB Office upon notification.
- Sign the receiving copy and receive your copy of the approved resolution or ordinance (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 7: Request for additional copies of official records (if needed)

- Submit a request form to the SB Secretariat to obtain additional copies of resolutions or ordinances.
- Staff searches for and releases the requested documents (approximately 10 minutes).

---

## Requirements

No specific documentary checklist is prescribed. Submit all relevant supporting documents together with your proposed ordinance or resolution. The SB Secretariat will advise on any additional attachments needed.

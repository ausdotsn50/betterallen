# Attend Disaster Preparedness Lectures — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (MDRRMO) provides disaster preparedness lectures and talks on DRRM-related topics for schools, barangays, and other organizations in Allen. A request must be submitted and approved before a lecture can be scheduled.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                          |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (MDRRMO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                               |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Government                                         |
| **Who May Avail**             | All agencies and organizations (schools, barangays, etc.)        |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 1 day, 4 hours, and 20 minutes (from request to lecture)         |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                             |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Visit the MDRRMO and sign the client logbook

- Go to the MDRRMO and sign in at the information desk logbook.
- Staff will conduct a brief interview to gather information about your request (approximately 6 minutes).

### Step 2: Submit your request letter

- Submit a request letter addressed to the Municipal Mayor, coursed through the Local Disaster Risk Reduction Management Officer (LDRRMO).
- Staff will check schedule availability and forward the letter to the Mayor's Office for approval (approximately 10 minutes for receipt; 1 day for LCE approval).
- Approval is subject to favorable weather conditions and availability of MDRRMO personnel.

### Step 3: Follow up after 1 day

- Return to the MDRRMO or call to follow up on the status of your request (approximately 4 minutes).
- If approved, the MDRRMO will set a schedule for the lecture.
- MDRRMO personnel will prepare the lecture materials (approximately 3 hours).

### Step 4: Receive the lecture on the scheduled date

- Wait for the MDRRMO lecturer on your agreed date and venue.
- The lecture will be conducted on disaster preparedness and other DRRM-related topics (approximately 1 hour, may vary depending on topic).

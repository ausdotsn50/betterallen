# Pay Market Stall Rental or Cemetery Lot Lease — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Treasurer's Office processes payments for market space rentals and public cemetery lot leases. Inquire with the Market Supervisor or Cemetery Caretaker first to confirm availability before proceeding to the MTO to pay.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                                      |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Treasurer's Office                                                 |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                           |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                                        |
| **Who May Avail**             | All individuals inquiring about or paying for market stalls or cemetery lots |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 20 minutes                                                                   |
| **Fees**                      | Based on the applicable rental or lease rate                                 |

---

## How to Avail

### For Market Stall Rental

### Step 1: Inquire with the Market Supervisor

- Ask the Market Supervisor whether a market space is available.
- The supervisor assists you and checks the requirements needed (approximately 10 minutes).

### Step 2: Receive your certificate and proceed to the MTO

- A certificate is issued and signed by the Mayor.
- Proceed to the MTO to pay the applicable fee (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 3: Proceed to the Mayor's Office for certification of ownership

- After payment, go to the Mayor's Office for the certification of ownership (approximately 5 minutes).

---

### For Cemetery Lot Lease

### Step 1: Inquire with the Cemetery Caretaker

- Ask the Cemetery Caretaker about available lots.
- The caretaker assists and issues a certificate signed by the Mayor.

### Step 2: Proceed to the MTO to pay

- Pay the applicable cemetery lot lease fee at the MTO (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 3: Proceed to the Mayor's Office

- Go to the Mayor's Office for the certification of ownership (approximately 5 minutes).

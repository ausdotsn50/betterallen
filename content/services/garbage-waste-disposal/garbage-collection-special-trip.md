# Request Special Garbage Collection — Municipality of Allen

Request on-demand garbage collection for urgent or special waste disposal needs beyond your regular schedule. This service is ideal for emergencies, large waste accumulation, or one-time collection situations.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                         |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Environment and Natural Resources Office (MENRO)      |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                              |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                           |
| **Who May Avail**             | Properties requiring emergency or additional garbage collection |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 20 hours _(including on-site inspection and travel)_            |
| **Fees**                      | ₱500.00 per trip                                                |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Request special collection service

- Contact the MENRO front desk
- Inquire about special trip garbage collection
- Submit request in person or by letter
- MENRO will schedule an on-site inspection

### Step 2: On-site inspection

- MENRO monitoring team conducts inspection at your property
- Team assesses waste volume, type, and segregation compliance
- After inspection, MENRO prepares the payment order

### Step 3: Pay fees at the Municipal Treasurer's Office

- Proceed to the MTO and pay ₱500.00 for the special trip
- Obtain your Official Receipt

### Step 4: Return to MENRO and schedule collection

- Return to MENRO with your Official Receipt
- Submit for endorsement and approval
- Confirm the collection date and time

### Step 5: Receive special collection service

- On the scheduled date, MENRO garbage collection team arrives at your property
- Conduct segregated waste collection and disposal
- Service complete

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Garbage collection slip _(issued by MENRO after inspection)_
- [ ] Official Receipt _(after payment at MTO)_
- [ ] Proper waste segregation at collection point

### Eligibility

- All property owners with special waste disposal needs
- Businesses and institutions requiring additional collection
- Properties with emergency waste situations

# Apply for Recruitment Activity Approval — Municipality of Allen

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) approves and facilitates recruitment activities — such as job fairs and on-site hiring events — for accredited local and overseas companies. These activities connect employers with qualified job seekers in Allen and Northern Samar.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                              |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Public Employment Service Office (PESO)                              |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                   |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                                |
| **Who May Avail**             | Employers and accredited companies (local and overseas)              |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 17 minutes (on-site); actual recruitment conducted on scheduled date |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                                 |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit a request to the PESO Office

- Visit the PESO Office or send your request letter by email to **lguallenpeso@gmail.com**.
- Staff will refer you to the assigned focal person (approximately 2 minutes).

### Step 2: Submit your requirements

- Present and submit all required documents.
- Staff checks completeness, settles the schedule and venue of the recruitment activity, and endorses the application to the PESO Manager (approximately 15 minutes).

### Step 3: PESO Manager approval

- The PESO Manager validates and approves the application.

### Step 4: Conduct the scheduled recruitment activity

- On the approved date, PESO staff arranges the venue and facilitates the recruitment activity.
- PESO assists applicants in accomplishing the National Skills Registration Program (NSRP) Form during the event.

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] 1 copy of Letter of Intent — _Company_
- [ ] 1 copy of Company Profile — _Company_
- [ ] 1 copy of Establishment Form — _PESO Office_
- [ ] 1 copy of National Skills Registry Program (NSRP) Form — _PESO Office_
- [ ] 1 copy of list of job vacancies with qualifications — _Company_
- [ ] 1 copy of Mayor's Permit — _Company_
- [ ] 1 copy of DTI or SEC Registration — _DTI or SEC_
- [ ] 1 copy of Certificate of No Pending Cases _(for local recruitment)_ — _DOLE_
- [ ] 1 copy of POEA License _(for overseas recruitment)_ — _POEA_
- [ ] 1 copy of approved Job Order _(for overseas recruitment)_ — _POEA_

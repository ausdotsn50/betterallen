# Access TUPAD Assistance for Displaced Workers — Municipality of Allen

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) facilitates enrollment in the Tulong Panghanapbuhay sa Ating Displaced/Disadvantaged Workers (TUPAD) program — a DOLE community-based safety net that provides temporary employment to workers in the informal sector, underemployed individuals, self-employed workers, and those who have lost their jobs or experienced reduced income.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Approach the PESO Information Desk

- Visit the PESO Office and inquire about the TUPAD program.
- Staff will refer you to the PESO focal person (approximately 2 minutes).

### Step 2: Submit initial requirements and undergo assessment

- Present your accomplished NSRP Form, Beneficiary Profile Form, Barangay Certificate of Indigency, and a valid ID.
- The PESO focal person will interview and assess if you qualify for the program (approximately 15 minutes).
- If qualified, staff will assist you in accomplishing the TUPAD forms, endorse you to your barangay, and conduct a short orientation about the program.

### Step 3: Fill out the Client Satisfaction Rating Form

- Accomplish and submit the Client Satisfaction Rating Form before leaving (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 4: Work as assigned and gather documentation

- Report to your assigned barangay and complete the 10-day work assignment.
- Gather all required documentation (barangay certificates, daily photos) for payroll processing.
- PESO staff will check completeness of documents and process your payroll.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] 1 copy of accomplished National Skills Registration Program (NSRP) Form — _PESO Office_
- [ ] 1 copy of Beneficiary Profile Form — _PESO Office_
- [ ] 1 original copy of Certificate of Indigency — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] 1 original copy of Barangay Certificate — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] 2 copies of valid ID with 3 specimen signatures — _Client/Beneficiary_
- [ ] 4 copies of Daily Time Record (DTR) signed by Punong Barangay or PESO Manager — _Barangay Hall / PESO Office_
- [ ] 1 copy of photos for documentation for each of the 10 working days — _Client/Beneficiary_

### Eligibility

- Underemployed workers, self-employed individuals, or displaced/marginalized workers residing in Allen.

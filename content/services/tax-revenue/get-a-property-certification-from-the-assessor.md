# Get a Property Certification from the Assessor — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Assessor's Office issues official certifications related to real property records for landowners who need documented proof of their property's status, classification, or assessment details. This may also involve a field inspection depending on the type of certification requested.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                        |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Assessor's Office                    |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                             |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                          |
| **Who May Avail**             | Landowners or their authorized representatives |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 3 days                                         |
| **Fees**                      | Certification Fee: ₱50.00                      |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your requirements to the front desk

- Submit all required documents to the Assessor's Office front desk.
- The Records Clerk researches the Property Record Form and forwards it to the Appraisal and Assessment Division.

### Step 2: Field inspection (if required)

- The Appraisal and Assessment Section may conduct a field visit to verify the property details relevant to the certification.

### Step 3: Preparation and approval of certification

- The Appraisal and Assessment Section prepares the required documents and forwards them to the Municipal Assessor's desk for recommending approval.
- The Records Clerk logs the transaction and the signed certification is forwarded to the front desk for release.

### Step 4: Proceed to the Provincial Assessor's Office (if required)

- Depending on the nature of the certification, you may be directed to proceed to the Provincial Assessor's Office for further action or approval.

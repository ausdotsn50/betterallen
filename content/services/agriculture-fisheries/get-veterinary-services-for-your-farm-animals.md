# Get Veterinary Services for Your Farm Animals — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) provides free veterinary services for livestock and poultry owners in Allen. Trained Livestock Technicians are available to assess, diagnose, and administer veterinary care to your farm animals.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                             |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO)                                  |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                  |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                               |
| **Who May Avail**             | All livestock and poultry owners in Allen                           |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 5 minutes (on-site consultation); field visits may take up to 1 day |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                                |

---

## How to Avail

### Step 1: Go to the MAO and request veterinary services

- Visit the MAO and inform the staff about your animal's condition or the veterinary service you need.
- A Trained Livestock Technician will interview you, take your animal's medical history, make a tentative diagnosis, and administer the necessary veterinary drug or treatment — if supplies are available (approximately 5 minutes for walk-in consultations; field visits may take up to 1 day).

---

## Requirements

No documentary requirements needed. Simply visit the MAO and describe your animal's condition.

---

## Services Available

- Animal health assessment and diagnosis
- Administration of veterinary drugs and treatment (subject to supply availability)
- Livestock and poultry health consultations

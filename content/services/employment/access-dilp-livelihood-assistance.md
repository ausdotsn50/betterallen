# Access DILP Livelihood Assistance — Municipality of Allen

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) facilitates access to the DOLE Integrated Livelihood Program (DILP), which provides grants to DOLE-registered associations and individuals to start or enhance livelihood undertakings. The program targets self-employed, low-paid, seasonal, and marginalized workers.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Approach the PESO Information Desk for inquiry

- Visit the PESO Office and inquire about the DILP program.
- Staff will refer you to the focal person and provide the list of requirements (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 2: Submit documents for evaluation

- Submit your DOLE Certificate of Registration and Business Proposal.
- Staff evaluates your submitted documents and assists associations without a business proposal (approximately 20 minutes).
- Complete documents are forwarded to DOLE.
- You will be advised to wait approximately **30 days** for the result of your application.

### Step 3: Wait for the result

- If approved, PESO prepares a purchase request and facilitates the procurement process once the fund is downloaded to the LGU (approximately 30 days).

### Step 4: Attend the turn-over ceremony (if approved)

- Attend the scheduled turn-over ceremony where the DILP assistance is formally handed over (approximately 2 hours).
- PESO staff arranges the venue and facilitates the ceremony.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] 1 original or photocopy of DOLE Certificate of Registration — _Association_
- [ ] 1 original copy of Business Proposal — _Association_

### Eligibility

- DOLE-registered associations or individuals who are self-employed, low-paid, seasonal, or marginalized workers.

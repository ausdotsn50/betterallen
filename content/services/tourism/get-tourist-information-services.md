# Get Tourist Information Services — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Tourism Office (MTO) provides tourism-related information and assistance to visitors, residents, and organizations. Inquiries may be made in person, by email, or through the official Tourism Facebook page.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                        |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Tourism Office (MTO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction             |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen          |
| **Who May Avail**             | All                            |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 15 minutes (walk-in)           |
| **Fees**                      | None                           |

---

## How to Avail

### For Walk-In Inquiries

### Step 1: Log in at the MTO and state your purpose

- Visit the Municipal Tourism Office and sign in at the office logbook.
- Staff assists you in filling out the logbook (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Receive assistance and information

- MTO personnel answers your inquiries and provides the tourism information you need (approximately 10 minutes).

---

### For Online Inquiries

- Send your inquiry by email or through the official Tourism Facebook page.
- The social media staff will reply as soon as the message is received or opened.

---

## Requirements

No documentary requirements needed. Simply visit the office or send your inquiry online.

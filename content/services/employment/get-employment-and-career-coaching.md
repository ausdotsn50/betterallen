# Get Employment and Career Coaching — Municipality of Allen

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) conducts Employment and Career Coaching sessions for Grade 10–12 students and graduating college students from public, private, and technical-vocational schools. Sessions cover career planning, resume writing, interview skills, and goal setting to help youth make informed career decisions.

## How to Request

### Step 1: Submit a request letter to the PESO Office

- The school submits a request letter to the PESO Office for an Employment or Career Coaching session.
- Staff receives the letter and confirms the schedule with the PESO focal person (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 2: Coordinate with PESO for the activity

- Coordinate with the PESO Office on the venue, date, and logistics.
- PESO staff prepares all necessary forms for the activity (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 3: Attend the coaching session on the scheduled date

- Students attend the Employment and Career Coaching session conducted by PESO staff (approximately 2 hours).
- Participants accomplish the National Skills Registration Program (NSRP) Form during the session.

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] 1 original copy of Request Letter from the school — _Public, Private, or Technical-Vocational School_
- [ ] 1 original copy of NSRP Form per participant — _PESO Office (provided during the session)_

### Eligibility

- Grade 10 to Grade 12 students and graduating college students enrolled in public, private, or technical-vocational schools.

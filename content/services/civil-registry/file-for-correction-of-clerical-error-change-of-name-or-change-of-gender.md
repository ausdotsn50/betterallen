# File for Correction of Clerical Error, Change of Name, or Change of Gender — Municipality of Allen

Under Republic Act No. 9048, the Municipal Civil Registrar has the authority to correct clerical or typographical errors in civil registry entries and to approve petitions for change of first name or nickname — without a court order. Petitions for change of gender/sex in the civil register may also be filed at this office.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Proceed to the Civil Registry Office and present your complete requirements

- Submit all required documents to the Municipal Civil Registrar.
- Staff receives, evaluates, and verifies requirements (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Pay at the Municipal Treasurer's Office

- Proceed to the MTO and pay the applicable petition fee.
- An Official Receipt will be issued (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 3: Return to the Civil Registry for processing

- Return and present your Official Receipt.
- The Civil Registrar prepares the petition papers (approximately 15 minutes), including:
  - Administering the oath of the petition
  - Preparing the Notice of Posting (for CCE) or Notice of Publication (for CFN and Change of Gender)
- After the posting or publication period, the Municipal Civil Registrar affirms or denies the petition.
- If affirmed, the petition and required attachments are transmitted to the PSA Central Office for final affirmation.

## Requirements

### A. Petition for Correction of Clerical Error (CCE)

- [ ] Certified machine copy of the certificate with the alleged erroneous entry
- [ ] At least 2 public or private documents supporting the correction
- [ ] Notice and Certificate of Posting
- [ ] Other documents as may be required by the Civil Registrar

### B. Petition for Change of First Name (CFN)

- [ ] All documents required for Correction of Clerical Error (above)
- [ ] Clearance or certification of no pending administrative, civil, or criminal case from:
  - Employer _(if employed)_
  - National Bureau of Investigation (NBI)
  - Philippine National Police (PNP)
- [ ] Affidavit of publication from the publisher and a copy of the newspaper clippings

### C. Petition for Change of Gender and Birth Day/Month

- [ ] All documents required for Correction of Clerical Error (above)
- [ ] Earliest school records or school documents
- [ ] Medical records
- [ ] Baptismal certificate or documents issued by religious authorities
- [ ] Clearance or certification of no pending case from employer (if employed), NBI, and PNP
- [ ] Medical certification from an accredited government physician confirming the petitioner has not undergone sex change or sex transplant
- [ ] Certificate of authenticity from the Civil Registrar on the physician's certification
- [ ] Affidavit of publication from the publisher and a copy of the newspaper clippings

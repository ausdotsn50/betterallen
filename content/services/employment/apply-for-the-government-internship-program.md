# Apply for the Government Internship Program — Municipality of Allen

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) facilitates enrollment in the Government Internship Program (GIP), a DOLE-administered program under KABATAAN 2000 (EO 139 s. 1993). It provides young workers — particularly poor and indigent youth — the opportunity to gain public service experience while supporting the implementation of DOLE programs at the local level.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Approach the PESO Information Desk

- Visit the PESO Office and inquire about the GIP.
- Staff will refer you to the PESO focal person (approximately 2 minutes).

### Step 2: Submit your documents and accomplish the GIP application form

- Present and submit all required documents to the PESO staff.
- Staff checks your documents, provides the GIP Application Form, and assists you in filling it out (approximately 30 minutes).
- Your GIP Identification Card will be processed.

### Step 3: Attend GIP orientation

- Attend the mandatory orientation where staff discusses the program's rules, reminders, and do's and don'ts (approximately 1 hour).

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] 4 copies of GIP Application Form — _DOLE Office or PESO_
- [ ] 4 copies of PSA Birth Certificate (photocopy) — _Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA)_
- [ ] 4 copies of Diploma, Transcript of Records, Form 137/138, or school certification (photocopy) — _School or University_
- [ ] 4 copies of 2×2 ID picture with collar on white background — _Computer shop_

### Eligibility

- Aged 18–30 years old (up to 35 for those in disaster-affected areas)
- Completed at least Senior High School, ALS equivalent, technical-vocational training, or college
- No prior work experience required

# Get a Certification of No Business Record — Municipality of Allen

The Business Permits and Licensing Section Office (BPLS) issues an official certification confirming that a particular individual or entity has no existing business record on file with the municipality.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                            |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Business Permits and Licensing Section Office      |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Business                             |
| **Who May Avail**             | Any applicant needing proof of no business record  |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 30 minutes                                         |
| **Fees**                      | Certification fee as indicated in the Revenue Code |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your letter of request

- Submit your written request letter to the BPLS frontline personnel.
- An Order of Payment will be issued for the certification fee.
- Proceed to the Municipal Treasurer's Office (MTO) cashier to pay.

### Step 2: Pay the required fee

- Pay the certification fee at the MTO cashier and receive your Official Receipt.

### Step 3: Present your receipt and claim the certification

- Return to the BPLS and present your Official Receipt.
- The BPLS Designate prepares the Certification of No Business Record.
- The Municipal Mayor signs and approves the certification.
- Claim your certified document from the frontline personnel.

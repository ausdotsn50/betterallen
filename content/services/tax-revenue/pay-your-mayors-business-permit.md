# Pay Your Mayor's Business Permit — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Treasurer's Office accepts payments for Mayor's Business Permit fees for both new applications and renewals. Payment is made at the MTO as part of the business permit process coordinated with the Business Permits and Licensing Section Office (BPLS).

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                             |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Treasurer's Office                        |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                  |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                               |
| **Who May Avail**             | All business permit applicants and permit holders   |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 15 minutes                                          |
| **Fees**                      | Based on declared gross sales or capital investment |

---

## How to Pay

### Step 1: Present your business documents at the MTO

- Bring your Sworn Statement of gross sales or capital investment, old permit, and CTC to the Revenue Collection Clerk.
- The clerk inspects the document requirements and begins computing your business tax bill (approximately 15 minutes total).

### Step 2: Computation of tax bill

- The clerk computes your tax bill based on your declared gross sales or capital investment.
- A CTC is required in relation to your gross sales or capital investment declaration.

### Step 3: Pay and have your application signed

- Pay the computed business tax fees.
- An Official Receipt is issued.
- Have your application form signed after payment.
- You will be instructed to proceed to the other offices concerned (e.g., BPLS) to complete the permit process.

# Request Property Reclassification — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Assessor's Office processes applications for the reclassification of real property. This involves a field inspection by the Appraisal and Assessment Section before a new Tax Declaration is prepared. Final approval is given by the Provincial Assessor's Office.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                        |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Assessor's Office                    |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                             |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                          |
| **Who May Avail**             | Landowners or their authorized representatives |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 3 days (at the municipal level)                |
| **Fees**                      | Miscellaneous Fee: ₱50.00                      |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your requirements to the front desk

- Submit all required documents to the Assessor's Office front desk.
- The Records Clerk researches the Property Record Form and forwards it to the Appraisal and Assessment Division.

### Step 2: Field inspection

- The Appraisal and Assessment Section conducts a field visit to inspect the land being applied for reclassification (part of the 3-day processing period).

### Step 3: Preparation of FAAS and Tax Declaration

- After the field inspection, the Appraisal and Assessment Section prepares the Field Appraisal and Assessment Sheet (FAAS) and updated Tax Declaration.
- Documents are forwarded to the Municipal Assessor's desk for recommending approval.

### Step 4: Recording and release

- The Records Clerk logs the transaction and forwards the signed Tax Declaration to the front desk for release.

### Step 5: Proceed to the Provincial Assessor's Office

- After receiving your documents, proceed to the Provincial Assessor's Office for final approval of the reclassification.

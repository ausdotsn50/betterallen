# Register a Birth, Death, or Marriage Document on Time — Municipality of Allen

All births, deaths, and marriages that occur within Allen, Northern Samar must be registered at the Municipal Civil Registry Office. Births and deaths must be registered within 30 days from the event. Ordinary marriages must be registered within 15 days, and marriages exempt from a license requirement within 30 days.

## How to Register

### Step 1: Proceed to the Municipal Civil Registry Office and present your documents

- Submit all required documents to the Civil Registry staff.
- Staff will receive, evaluate, and verify the requirements (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Pay at the Municipal Treasurer's Office

- Proceed to the MTO and pay the registration fee of ₱50.00.
- An Official Receipt will be issued.

### Step 3: Return to the Civil Registry, present your Official Receipt, and receive your document

- Return to the Civil Registry Office and present your Official Receipt.
- Staff assigns a Registry Number to the document (approximately 1 minute).
- The Municipal Civil Registrar registers the civil registry document (approximately 2 minutes).
- Your registered civil registry document is released (approximately 1 minute).

---

## Requirements

### For **Birth** Registration

- [ ] 4 copies of Certificate of Live Birth (COLB)
- [ ] Certificate of Marriage of the parents _(for marital births)_
- [ ] Affidavit to Use the Surname of the Father (AUSF) _(for non-marital births)_

### For **Death** Registration

- [ ] 4 copies of Certificate of Death (COD)
- [ ] Certificate from the Barangay and Local Health Office _(if the person died outside a hospital, clinic, or at home)_

### For **Marriage** Registration

- [ ] 4 copies of Certificate of Marriage
- [ ] Marriage License
- [ ] Affidavit exempted from Marriage License _(if applicable)_

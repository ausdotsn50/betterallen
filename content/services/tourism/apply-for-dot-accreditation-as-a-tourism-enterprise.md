# Apply for DOT Accreditation as a Tourism Enterprise — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Tourism Office (MTO) assists tourism-related businesses and enterprises in creating their Department of Tourism (DOT) accreditation accounts and completing online registration at the DOT accreditation portal. Both new applicants and businesses due for renewal can coordinate with the MTO for assistance.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                   |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Tourism Office (MTO)                            |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                        |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Business                                    |
| **Who May Avail**             | All tourism-related enterprises and businesses in Allen   |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 5–15 minutes (MTO-assisted online registration)           |
| **Fees**                      | None (MTO assistance is free; DOT may have separate fees) |

---

## How to Apply

### For New Applicants

### Step 1: Coordinate with the MTO for a schedule

- Contact or visit the MTO to schedule your assisted online registration session.

### Step 2: Online registration at the DOT accreditation portal

- The Tourism Officer logs in to the DOT Accreditation page at **accreditation.tourism.gov.ph**.
- Staff assists you in selecting the type of entity and answering the registration questions (approximately 5–15 minutes).
- The online registration form is filled out and submitted.

### Step 3: Wait for DOT confirmation

- DOT will send a confirmation or status update to your registered email address.

---

### For Renewal Applicants

### Step 1: Coordinate with the MTO for a schedule

- Contact or visit the MTO to schedule your assisted renewal session.

### Step 2: Online renewal at the DOT accreditation portal

- The Tourism Officer logs in to the DOT Accreditation page at **accreditation.tourism.gov.ph**.
- Staff assists you in updating your profile details (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 3: Wait for DOT confirmation

- DOT will send a confirmation or status update to your registered email address.

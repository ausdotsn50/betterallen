# Get a Certified Copy of Your Tax Declaration — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Assessor's Office issues certified true copies and photocopies of Tax Declarations for landowners or their authorized representatives. These documents are commonly required for property transactions, loan applications, and other legal purposes.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                        |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Assessor's Office                    |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                             |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                          |
| **Who May Avail**             | Landowners or their authorized representatives |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 10–15 minutes                                  |
| **Fees**                      | ₱50.00 (Certification Fee)                     |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your requirements to the front desk

- Submit all required documents to the Assessor's Office front desk officer.
- The Records Clerk researches and photocopies the requested Tax Declaration and forwards it to the Municipal Assessor's desk (approximately 10–15 minutes).

### Step 2: Signing and recording

- The Municipal Assessor affixes their signature on the Tax Declaration and forwards it to the Records Section for recording.
- The Records Clerk logs the transaction in the logbook.

### Step 3: Claim your certified copy

- The front desk officer releases your certified Tax Declaration.

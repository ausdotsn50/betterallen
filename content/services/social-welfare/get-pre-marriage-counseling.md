# Get Pre-Marriage Counseling — Municipality of Allen

The MSWDO conducts Pre-Marriage Orientation and Counseling (PMOC) sessions for would-be couples as a required step before securing a marriage license. Attending PMOC is mandatory under the Family Code of the Philippines.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                 |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office (MSWDO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                      |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                   |
| **Who May Avail**             | Would-be couples applying for a marriage license        |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Approximately 1 hour and 23 minutes                     |
| **Fees**                      | ₱400.00                                                 |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Apply for PMOC at the MSWDO

- Visit the MSWDO and register as a couple applying for pre-marriage counseling.
- Staff records your details in the client logbook.
- An intake interview is conducted (approximately 20 minutes).

### Step 2: Attend the PMOC session

- Participate in the Pre-Marriage Orientation and Counseling session (approximately 1 hour).
- Topics cover responsibilities, family planning, and life in marriage.

### Step 3: Receive your Certificate of Compliance

- After completing the session, receive your Certificate of Compliance or Certificate of Marriage Counseling.
- Present this certificate when applying for your marriage license at the Local Civil Registrar.

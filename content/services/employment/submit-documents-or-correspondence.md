# Submit Documents or Correspondence — Municipality of Allen

The Human Resource Management Office (HRMO) receives HR-related documents and correspondence from employees and the general public. All submissions are reviewed and acted upon in accordance with existing HRMO procedures.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                          |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Office**                    | Human Resource Management Office (HRMO)          |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                               |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen / Government to Government |
| **Who May Avail**             | All employees and members of the public          |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 10 minutes                                       |
| **Fees**                      | None                                             |

---

## How to Submit

### Step 1: Accomplish the Action Slip and submit your documents

- Secure an Action Slip from the Office of the Municipal Mayor.
- Submit your document or correspondence together with the Action Slip and any supporting documents to the HRMO.
- The HRMO receives and endorses the submission (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: HRMO reviews and takes appropriate action

- The HRMO staff reviews the documents and provides the appropriate action (approximately 5 minutes).

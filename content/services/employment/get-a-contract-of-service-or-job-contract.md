# Get a Contract of Service or Job Order Contract — Municipality of Allen

The Human Resource Management Office (HRMO) prepares and issues Contracts of Service and Job Order Contracts for individuals engaged by the Local Government Unit of Allen for specific projects or short-duration work. These contracts are processed through the Mayor's Office and require budget and position availability clearances before signing.

---

| Information                   | Details                                                   |

| **Estimated Processing Time** | 2 hours and 30 minutes (if all signatories are available) |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                      |

---

## How to Process

### Step 1: Submit a Letter of Request or Recommendation to the Mayor's Office

- The requesting Department Head or client submits a Letter of Intent or Recommendation to the Office of the Municipal Mayor.
- The Mayor's Office receives and reviews the document (approximately 15 minutes).

### Step 2: Budget and position availability checks

- The Municipal Budget Officer checks the availability of budget (approximately 15 minutes).
- The HRMO checks the availability of the position (approximately 15 minutes).
- The Municipal Mayor or Head of Agency approves and endorses to the HRMO (approximately 10 minutes).

### Step 3: Contract preparation

- The HRMO prepares the Contract of Service or Job Order Contract for signing (approximately 15 minutes).

### Step 4: Sign the contract and complete certifications

- The individual signs the contract and submits required documents.
- HRMO certifies availability of position (approximately 10 minutes).
- Budget Officer certifies availability of budget (approximately 15 minutes).
- For Contract of Service: the Municipal Administrator certifies the duties and qualifications of the hire (approximately 15 minutes).
- The Municipal Mayor signs on behalf of the LGU (approximately 20 minutes).
- For Contract of Service: the HRMO acknowledges and notarizes the document (approximately 15 minutes).

### Step 5: Receive your certified copy

- The HRMO prepares and issues your certified copy of the Contract of Service or Job Order Contract (approximately 15 minutes).

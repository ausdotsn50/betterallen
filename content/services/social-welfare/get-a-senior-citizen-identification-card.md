# Get a Senior Citizen Identification Card — Municipality of Allen

The Office of the Senior Citizen Affairs (OSCA) under the MSWDO issues senior citizen ID cards to residents of Allen who are 60 years old and above. The OSCA ID grants access to discounts, benefits, and privileges under the Expanded Senior Citizens Act.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                    |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Office of the Senior Citizen Affairs (OSCA) / MSWDO        |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                         |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                      |
| **Who May Avail**             | Senior citizens (60 years old and above) residing in Allen |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 10 minutes                                                 |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                       |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your documentary requirements

- Submit all required documents to the OSCA/MSWDO staff.
- Staff will check completeness of submitted documents.
- Your OSCA ID will be prepared.

### Step 2: Receive your Senior Citizen ID card

- Staff records the transaction in the logbook.
- Receive your OSCA ID card.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Accomplished application form — _OSCA/MSWDO_
- [ ] Birth Certificate (PSA or Local Civil Registry copy)
- [ ] 1×1 ID picture

# Request a Job Posting — Municipality of Allen

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) posts job vacancies from accredited companies on the PESO Allen official Facebook page and in the Public Employment Information System (PEIS) to reach job seekers in Allen and the surrounding area. Employers can submit job postings by walk-in or email.

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your job posting request

- Walk in to the PESO Office or send your request by email to **lguallenpeso@gmail.com**, including all job vacancy details and qualification requirements.
- PESO staff receives your request letter and provides the Establishment Form (approximately 2 minutes).

### Step 2: Accomplish and submit the Establishment Form

- Fill out the Establishment Form completely.
- Staff receives the accomplished form and encodes the vacancy details into the Public Employment Information System (PEIS) (approximately 18 minutes).
- The job vacancy is posted on the PESO Allen official Facebook page for wide dissemination.

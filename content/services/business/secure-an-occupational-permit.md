# Secure an Occupational Permit — Municipality of Allen

The Business Permits and Licensing Section Office (BPLS) issues Occupational Permits to employees as required by business employers in the municipality.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                               |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Business Permits and Licensing Section Office                         |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                    |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Business                                                |
| **Who May Avail**             | Employees required to secure an occupational permit by their employer |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 30 minutes                                                            |
| **Fees**                      | Applicable fees as per Revenue Code                                   |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your requirements to BPLS

- Submit all required documents to the BPLS frontline personnel.
- Staff will check completeness of requirements and encode your details.
- You will be informed to proceed to the cashier.
- Staff prints and forwards the Occupational Permit for signature and Municipal Mayor's approval.

### Step 2: Pay the required fees

- Pay at the MTO cashier and receive your Official Receipt.

### Step 3: Claim your Occupational Permit

- Return to the BPLS and present your Official Receipt.
- Staff records the transaction and releases your Occupational Permit.

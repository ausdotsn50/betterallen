# Request a Social Case Study Report — Municipality of Allen

The MSWDO prepares Social Case Study Reports for residents who need documentation of their socio-economic situation to access financial assistance, medicines, hospital services, or other support from government agencies and partner organizations.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                 |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office (MSWDO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                      |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Government / Government to Citizen        |
| **Who May Avail**             | All citizens of Allen                                   |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Approximately 3–7 hours (same day for most cases)       |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                    |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Request the report at the MSWDO

- Visit the MSWDO and explain the purpose of your request (e.g., for hospital assistance, medicine, or referral to another agency).
- Staff records your details in the client logbook.
- Your documents are verified (approximately 10 minutes).

### Step 2: Undergo an intake interview

- The Social Worker conducts an interview to gather information about your situation (approximately 10–20 minutes).

### Step 3: Social Case Study Report preparation

- The Social Worker prepares your Social Case Study Report (approximately 3–6 hours).

### Step 4: Receive your Social Case Study Report

- The completed report is issued to you (approximately 5 minutes).

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Certificate of Indigency — _Concerned Barangay_
- [ ] Medical Certificate _(if for medical or hospital assistance)_ — _Hospital or Clinic_
- [ ] Death Certificate _(if for burial assistance)_ — _Local Civil Registry_
- [ ] Proof of billing _(as applicable)_ — _Hospital or Clinic_
- [ ] Request letter or referral from the referring agency _(if applicable)_

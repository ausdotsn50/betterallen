# Apply for Leave — Municipality of Allen

The Human Resource Management Office (HRMO) processes leave applications for incumbent officials and employees of the Local Government Unit of Allen. All leave applications must be approved by the concerned Department Head and the Local Chief Executive before they take effect.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                            |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Human Resource Management Office (HRMO)            |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Government                           |
| **Who May Avail**             | Incumbent officials and employees of the LGU Allen |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 25 minutes                                         |
| **Fees**                      | None                                               |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Request and accomplish the Application for Leave Form

- Request the Application for Leave Form from the HRMO (approximately 5 minutes).
- Fill out the form completely.

### Step 2: Submit the form to the HRMO after Department Head approval

- Have your Department Head sign the form first.
- Submit the approved form to the HRMO.
- Staff receives the form, checks the type of leave being applied for, and certifies your total leave balance (approximately 10 minutes).

### Step 3: Receive your copy for the LCE's approval

- HRMO gives you your copy of the form for submission to the Local Chief Executive (Municipal Mayor) for final approval (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 4: Proceed to the Mayor's Office for LCE approval

- Bring the form to the Mayor's Office for the Mayor's approval (approximately 5 minutes).
- Return to the HRMO and submit the approved form.

### Step 5: Return to the HRMO

- The HRMO files the approved Application for Leave Form for record (approximately 2 minutes).

# Transfer Property Ownership or Tax Declaration — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Assessor's Office processes the transfer of Tax Declaration from one owner to another following a sale, donation, or other conveyance of real property. After processing at the municipal level, the transfer must be approved by the Provincial Assessor's Office.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                            |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Assessor's Office                        |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                              |
| **Who May Avail**             | Landowners or their authorized representatives     |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 3 days (at the municipal level)                    |
| **Fees**                      | Miscellaneous Fee — _Municipal Treasurer's Office_ |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your requirements to the front desk

- Submit all required documents to the Assessor's Office front desk.
- The Records Clerk researches the Property Record Form and forwards it to the Assessment Section.

### Step 2: Preparation of FAAS and Tax Declaration

- The Assessment Clerk prepares the Field Appraisal and Assessment Sheet (FAAS) and new Tax Declaration, together with the attached requirements (approximately 3 days).
- Documents are forwarded to the Municipal Assessor's desk for recommending approval.

### Step 3: Recording and release

- The Records Clerk logs the transaction in the logbook and forwards the signed Tax Declaration to the front desk for release.

### Step 4: Proceed to the Provincial Assessor's Office

- After receiving your documents from the Municipal Assessor's Office, proceed to the Provincial Assessor's Office for final approval of the transfer.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] 2 photocopies of the Registered Deed of Conveyance
- [ ] 2 Certified True Copies of the Title
- [ ] Photocopy of Transfer Tax Receipt — _Provincial Treasurer's Office (PTO)_
- [ ] Certificate Authorizing Registration (CAR) — _Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR)_
- [ ] DAR Clearance _(if agricultural land)_
- [ ] Real Property Tax Clearance — _Municipal Treasurer's Office_
- [ ] Miscellaneous Fee — _Municipal Treasurer's Office_
- [ ] Previous Tax Declaration

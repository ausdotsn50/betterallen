# Renew Your Business Permit — Municipality of Allen

All business owners must renew their Business or Mayor's Permit every year. Renewal is accepted from **January 1 to 20**. Penalties apply after this period. Business taxes are computed based on gross receipts or sales.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                                         |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Business Permits and Licensing Section Office                                   |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                              |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Business                                                          |
| **Who May Avail**             | All existing business permit holders due for renewal                            |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 1 day (with complete requirements)                                              |
| **Fees**                      | Based on gross receipts or sales; payable annually, semi-annually, or quarterly |
| **Renewal Deadline**          | January 1–20 each year (penalties apply after January 20)                       |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Secure and submit the Unified Application Form

- Get the Unified Application Form from the BPLS Office.
- Accomplish the form completely and attach all required documents.
- Submit to the BPLS Staff-in-Charge for completeness verification.

### Step 2: Receive your Order of Payment

- BPLS staff prepares and signs your Order of Payment based on assessment.
- You will receive the signed Order of Payment and a copy of your application form.
- Proceed to the Municipal Treasurer's Office (MTO) to pay.

### Step 3: Pay fees, submit receipt, and claim your renewed Business Permit

- Pay the required business tax fees at the MTO cashier.
- Return your Official Receipt to the BPLS receiving section.
- Affix your signature in the logbook and claim your renewed Business Permit.

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Proof of Registration (SEC for corporations, CDA for cooperatives, DTI for sole proprietorships)
- [ ] Proof of Annual Gross Receipts: Audited Financial Statement, Sworn Statement of Gross Receipts, or Income Tax Return
- [ ] Fire Safety Inspection Certificate — _Bureau of Fire Protection_
- [ ] Sanitary Permit or Sanitary Clearance — _Municipal Health Office_
- [ ] Real Property Clearance — _Municipal Treasurer's Office_
- [ ] Barangay Clearance — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] Municipal Solid Waste Compliance Certificate — _MENRO_
- [ ] Anti-Fencing Certificate _(for junkshops only)_ — _Philippine National Police_
- [ ] Certificate of Tax Exemption _(for hospitals, schools, gasoline stations, non-profits, cooperatives, dental clinics)_
- [ ] The following are required **only if not submitted during initial application**:
  - Location plan or sketch of business premises
  - Occupancy Permit — _Building Official_
  - Location Clearance — _Municipal Planning and Development Office_
  - Proof of right to use business location (ownership or lease documents)
  - ECC or CNC Registration — _DENR_
- [ ] Other clearances or permits from national agencies as required by line of business

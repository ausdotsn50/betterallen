# Access Free Inbred and Hybrid Seeds Through RCEF — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) distributes free inbred and hybrid rice seeds to registered farmers under the Rice Competitiveness Enhancement Fund (RCEF) program. Seeds are released after a mandatory orientation on rice farming best practices.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                             |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO)                  |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                  |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                               |
| **Who May Avail**             | All registered rice farmers in the RSBSA Masterlist |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 35 minutes                                          |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                |

---

## How to Avail

### Step 1: Proceed to the MAO before seed release

- Before seeds are distributed, attend a mandatory orientation on the Palaycheck system and updates on the rice program conducted by the MAO (approximately 30 minutes).

### Step 2: Verification and seed release

- MAO staff will check the RSBSA Masterlist to confirm your registration.
- If listed, you will be interviewed, have your photo taken, and receive your seeds (approximately 5 minutes).

---

## Requirements

- You must be registered in the **RSBSA (Registry System for Basic Sector in Agriculture)** to qualify.
- If you are not yet registered, visit the MAO first to enroll before the seed distribution schedule.

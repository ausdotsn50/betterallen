# Get a Tax Clearance or Certificate — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Treasurer's Office issues Tax Clearance Certificates to property owners who have settled their real property tax obligations. A Tax Clearance is commonly required for business permits, property transactions, and other government processes.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                 |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Treasurer's Office            |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                      |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                   |
| **Who May Avail**             | All taxpayers                           |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 10 minutes                              |
| **Fees**                      | Confirm with the MTO (per Revenue Code) |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Present your payment records at the MTO

- Bring your previous Official Receipt and Index Card to the MTO.
- The Revenue Collection Clerk looks up your payment records and verifies that your taxes are up to date (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Issuance and signing of Tax Clearance

- The clerk types and issues the Tax Clearance (approximately 5 minutes).
- The Municipal Treasurer signs the Tax Clearance and records the transaction in the logbook.
- Receive your signed Tax Clearance Certificate.

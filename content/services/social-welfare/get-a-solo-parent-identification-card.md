# Get a Solo Parent Identification Card — Municipality of Allen

The MSWDO issues Solo Parent ID cards to qualified residents of Allen under Republic Act No. 8972 (Solo Parents' Welfare Act). The ID entitles solo parents to access support programs and government assistance.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                 |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office (MSWDO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                      |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                   |
| **Who May Avail**             | Solo parents residing in Allen                          |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Within the day (upon completion of requirements)        |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                    |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your application form and supporting documents

- Get and accomplish the application form from the MSWDO.
- Submit together with all supporting documents.
- Staff records your details in the client logbook.

### Step 2: Attend a brief orientation and document verification

- A Social Worker conducts a brief discussion of available solo parent services and their requirements (approximately 10–15 minutes).
- Your submitted documents are verified (approximately 20 minutes).

### Step 3: Receive your Solo Parent ID card

- Once your application is verified and complete, your Solo Parent ID card is issued on the same day.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Accomplished application form — _MSWDO_
- [ ] Birth Certificate of child or children below 18 years old — _Local Civil Registry or PSA_
- [ ] Proof of being a solo parent _(requirements vary by category)_:
  - Death Certificate of deceased spouse
  - Court Order for annulment or legal separation
  - Sworn Affidavit
  - Medical Certificate
  - Police or Barangay Record
  - Other supporting documents as applicable

### Eligibility

- Residents of Allen who qualify as solo parents under RA 8972, including those who are unmarried, separated, widowed, or whose spouse is incapacitated or detained.

# Get Free Insurance for Crops, Livestock, and Fisheries — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) facilitates free government insurance coverage for registered farmers and fisherfolk in Allen. Coverage is available for rice, high-value crops, livestock, and fisheries through the Philippine Crop Insurance Corporation (PCIC) and related programs.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                       |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO)                            |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                            |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                         |
| **Who May Avail**             | All registered farmers and fisherfolk in the RSBSA Masterlist |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 15 minutes (initial application at the MAO)                   |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                          |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Proceed to the MAO to apply for insurance

- Visit the MAO and inform the staff that you would like to apply for crop, livestock, or fisheries insurance.
- Staff will check the RSBSA Masterlist to verify your registration.
- If you are listed, the MAO Focal Person will conduct an interview to gather relevant information about your farm or fishing operation (approximately 15 minutes).

---

## Requirements

- You must be registered in the **RSBSA (Registry System for Basic Sector in Agriculture)** to qualify.
- If you are not yet registered, visit the MAO first to enroll in the RSBSA before applying for insurance.

---

## Coverage Areas

- **Rice and other crops** — handled by the Rice and HVCDP Focal Persons
- **Livestock** — handled by the Livestock Focal Person
- **Fisheries** — handled by the Fisheries Focal Person

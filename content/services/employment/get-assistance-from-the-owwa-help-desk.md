# Get Assistance from the OWWA Help Desk — Municipality of Allen

The PESO Office hosts an Overseas Workers Welfare Administration (OWWA) Help Desk to attend to the concerns of Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) and their families, and to provide information on overseas employment and OWWA services. Only active OWWA members may avail of OWWA services through the help desk.

## How to Avail

### Step 1: Approach the PESO Information Desk

- Visit the PESO Office and inquire about OWWA services.
- Staff will refer you to the focal person.
- Staff verifies if you are an active OWWA member. If you are a member, they will inform you of available OWWA services and provide an OFW Information Sheet.
- **Note:** Non-OWWA members cannot avail of OWWA services through this help desk (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 2: Fill out the OFW Information Sheet

- Accomplish and submit the OFW Information Sheet.
- Staff assists and receives the completed form, conducts an initial assessment of your case if needed, and undertakes monitoring and evaluation (approximately 20 minutes).

### Step 3: Follow up on your case

- Staff prepares case summary reports and updates the client database.
- Your application is endorsed to the OWWA Regional Welfare Office (RWO) for further assistance and evaluation (may take up to 30 days).

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] 1 original copy of OFW Information Sheet — _PESO Office_
- [ ] 1 photocopy of proof of OFW or OWWA membership — _OWWA_
- [ ] 1 photocopy of Overseas Employment Certificate (OEC) — _POEA_
- [ ] Termination document _(if applicable)_ — _Applicant_
- [ ] Photocopy of passport (2nd page with photo and number, plus latest departure and arrival stamps) — _Applicant_

### Eligibility

- Active OFWs with valid OWWA membership residing in or from Allen.

# Apply for Financial Assistance During Crisis Situations — Municipality of Allen

The MSWDO provides financial assistance to indigent individuals and families facing crisis situations through the Assistance to Individuals in Crisis Situations (AICS) program. This covers medical, burial, transportation, and emergency shelter assistance due to natural or man-made calamities.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                 |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office (MSWDO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                      |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                   |
| **Who May Avail**             | Indigent individuals and families in crisis situations  |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Approximately 3–5 hours (same-day processing)           |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                    |

---

## Types of Assistance Available

- **Medical Assistance** — for hospitalization, medicines, laboratory, or doctor's fees
- **Burial Assistance** — for the death of an indigent family member
- **Transportation Assistance** — for medical referrals and transfers
- **Emergency Shelter Assistance (ESA)** — for families whose homes were destroyed by fire, typhoon, or other calamities

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Request assistance at the MSWDO

- Approach the MSWDO and state the nature of your request.
- Staff records your details in the client logbook.
- Your documents are verified and an intake interview is conducted (approximately 10–20 minutes).

### Step 2: Processing of Certificate of Eligibility and vouchers

- The Social Worker prepares a Certificate of Eligibility and assistance vouchers and submits for review and recommendation (approximately 10–20 minutes).
- The documents are forwarded for signature to the Budget Office, Accounting Office, and Mayor's Office (approximately 3–4 hours).

### Step 3: Receive your financial assistance

- Proceed to the Municipal Treasurer's Office to receive the financial assistance (approximately 20 minutes).

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Certificate of Indigency — _Office of the Punong Barangay_
- [ ] For **medical assistance**: Medical Certificate, doctor's prescription, laboratory request, or hospital bill — _Hospital, Clinic, or MHO_
- [ ] For **transportation assistance**: Two-way referral — _Hospital, Clinic, or MHO_
- [ ] For **burial assistance**: Death Certificate — _Local Civil Registry_
- [ ] For **emergency shelter assistance**: Barangay Incident Report, Police Report, or Fire Certification — _Punong Barangay, PNP, or BFP_

### Eligibility

- Indigent or low-income residents of Allen who are currently in a crisis situation.

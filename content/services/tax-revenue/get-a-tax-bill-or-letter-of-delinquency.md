# Get a Tax Bill or Letter of Delinquency — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Treasurer's Office prepares and issues Tax Bills and Letters of Delinquency for real property taxpayers with current or outstanding unpaid balances. These documents show the amount owed and serve as official notices for delinquent accounts.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                      |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Treasurer's Office |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction           |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen        |
| **Who May Avail**             | All real property taxpayers  |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 5 minutes                    |
| **Fees**                      | None stated                  |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Request your Tax Bill or Letter of Delinquency at the MTO

- Approach the Revenue Collection Clerk at the MTO.
- The clerk computes your current and delinquent real property tax balances using your Index Card.
- Your Tax Bill and/or Letter of Delinquency is issued (approximately 5 minutes).

# Access Welfare Programs for Socially Disadvantaged Women — Municipality of Allen

The MSWDO provides psycho-social support, case management, and access to intervention services for abused women, VAWC (Violence Against Women and their Children) victims, and women in especially difficult circumstances.

---

| **Who May Avail**             | Abused women, VAWC victims, and women in especially difficult circumstances          |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Intake and initial assessment: same day; case management may span 6 months to 1 year |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                                                 |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Seek assistance at the MSWDO

- Approach the MSWDO or be referred through the VAWC Desk, Punong Barangay, or PNP.
- Staff records your details in the client logbook.
- An intake interview and verification of submitted reports is conducted (approximately 1 hour or more).

### Step 2: Undergo assessment and treatment planning

- A Social Worker conducts assessment, evaluation, and prepares a Social Case Study Report with a treatment plan (approximately 2–4 hours).
- A home visitation may be scheduled as needed.

### Step 3: Access intervention services and case management

- Your treatment plan is implemented and monitored over an appropriate period (case management may last 6 months to 1 year).
- Referrals to partner agencies are arranged as needed.
- The Social Worker evaluates the case for termination once goals are achieved and prepares a Terminal Report.

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Barangay or Police Report — _Barangay Hall or PNP_
- [ ] Referral letter _(if referred by a VAWC Desk officer, Punong Barangay, or other agency)_

### Eligibility

- Women who are victims of abuse, VAWC, or in especially difficult circumstances residing in or found within Allen.

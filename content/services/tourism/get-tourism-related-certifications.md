# Get Tourism-Related Certifications — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Tourism Office (MTO) issues tourism-related certifications requested by partner agencies, organizations, and other concerned parties. These certifications are issued upon formal request and subject to the availability and approval of the Municipal Tourism Officer.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                            |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Tourism Office (MTO)                     |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen / Non-Government Agencies    |
| **Who May Avail**             | Concerned agencies, organizations, and individuals |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 18 minutes                                         |
| **Fees**                      | None                                               |

---

## How to Request

### Step 1: Submit your request letter or fill out the Request Form

- Submit a Letter Request addressed to the Municipal Mayor and the Municipal Tourism Officer, clearly indicating the certification needed and the purpose of the request.
- Alternatively, fill out the MTO Request Form completely.
- Staff accepts and records your request (approximately 1 minute).

### Step 2: Review and preparation of certification

- The Tourism Officer reviews, approves, and prepares the requested certification (approximately 10 minutes).
- The certification is recorded in the office log (approximately 1 minute).

### Step 3: Release and coordination

- The certification is released to you (approximately 1 minute).
- The Tourism Officer coordinates with you on the details or any follow-up as needed (approximately 5 minutes).

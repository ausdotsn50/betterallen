# Request Tourism Data — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Tourism Office (MTO) processes requests for tourism-related data and statistics. Data requests may be submitted through a formal letter addressed to the Municipal Mayor and Tourism Officer, or by filling out the MTO's Data Request Form.

---

| Information                   | Details                                             |

---

## How to Request

### Step 1: Submit your request letter or fill out the Data Request Form

- Submit a Letter Request addressed to the Municipal Mayor and the Municipal Tourism Officer, clearly indicating the specific data needed and the purpose of the request.
- Alternatively, fill out the MTO Data Request Form completely.

### Step 2: MTO checks data availability

- MTO Data Personnel checks whether the requested data is available in the MTO system (approximately 4 minutes).
- If some or all of the requested data are not available at the MTO, staff will provide contact details of the agency where the data can be obtained (approximately 3 minutes).

### Step 3: Data extraction and release

- If the data is available, staff extracts it from the MTO system (approximately 10 minutes).
- The requested data is released to you (approximately 3 minutes).

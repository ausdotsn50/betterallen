# Get Technical Assistance on Crop Production and Management — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) provides free technical guidance to farmers on crop production, fertilization, pest management, and post-harvest practices. Walk in anytime during office hours — no prior appointment needed.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                            |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen              |
| **Who May Avail**             | All farmers and residents of Allen |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 30 minutes                         |
| **Fees**                      | None                               |

---

## How to Avail

### Step 1: Go to the MAO and request technical assistance

- Visit the MAO and tell the staff what kind of assistance you need (planting, fertilization, pest management, or post-harvest).
- MAO staff will answer your queries and provide guidance (approximately 15 minutes).

### Step 2: Fill out the client satisfaction form

- Accomplish the client satisfaction form before leaving (approximately 15 minutes).

---

## Requirements

No prior documentary requirements needed. Simply visit the MAO during office hours.

---

## Topics Covered

- Crop planting and management
- Fertilization schedules and techniques
- Pest and disease management
- Post-harvest handling and practices

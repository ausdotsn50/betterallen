# Get a Certificate of Employment — Municipality of Allen

The Human Resource Management Office (HRMO) issues Certificates of Employment to current and former personnel of the Local Government Unit of Allen. The certificate includes the employee's employment history with the LGU, covering regular, Job Order, and Contract of Service engagements.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                    |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Human Resource Management Office (HRMO)                    |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                         |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Government                                   |
| **Who May Avail**             | LGU Allen personnel and employees (current and former)     |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 30 minutes to 1 day (depending on availability of records) |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                       |

---

## How to Request

### Step 1: Accomplish and submit a Request Form Slip

- Get and accomplish the Request Form Slip at the HRMO.
- Submit together with your ID or authorization letter if applicable.
- Staff receives and reviews your request (part of the processing period).

### Step 2: HRMO retrieves records and prepares the certificate

- Staff retrieves your 201 file and Leave Card to verify your employment records.
- The Certificate of Employment is prepared and signed (within 30 minutes to 1 day, depending on record availability).

### Step 3: Receive your Certificate of Employment

- The signed Certificate of Employment is released to you.

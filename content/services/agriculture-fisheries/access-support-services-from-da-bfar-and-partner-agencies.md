# Access Support Services from DA, BFAR, and Partner Agencies — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) serves as a gateway for farmers, fisherfolk, rural improvement clubs (RICs), and 4H associations to access programs and support services from the Department of Agriculture (DA), Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources (BFAR) Regional Field Office 8, and other partner agencies.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                 |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO)                      |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                      |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                   |
| **Who May Avail**             | Farmers, fisherfolk, RICs, and 4H associations in Allen |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Approximately 9 days (from inquiry to endorsement)      |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                    |

---

## How to Avail

### Step 1: Go to the MAO for inquiry

- Visit the MAO and present your request or inquiry about the support service you need.
- The Municipal Agriculturist or Rural-Based Organizations (RBO) Focal Person will validate the legitimacy of your request (approximately 1 day).

### Step 2: Document preparation

- MAO staff will assist you in preparing all necessary documents required by the partner agency (approximately 1 week).

### Step 3: Endorsement to the concerned agency

- MAO formally endorses your request with complete documents to DA, BFAR RFO8, or the appropriate partner agency (approximately 1 day).

---

## Requirements

Requirements vary depending on the specific program or service being requested. MAO staff will guide you on what documents to prepare after your initial inquiry.

---

## Types of Support Available

- Agricultural inputs and equipment programs (DA)
- Fisheries programs and livelihood support (BFAR RFO8)
- Farmer and fisherfolk organization development programs
- Rural improvement club and 4H youth programs
- Other DA/BFAR regional programs as available

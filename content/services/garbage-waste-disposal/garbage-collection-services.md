# Secure Garbage Collection Services — Municipality of Allen

Secure regular garbage collection services for your residential, commercial, institutional, or industrial property. Services follow Ecological Solid Waste Management (ESWM) standards under Republic Act 9003 with proper waste segregation and timely collection.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                             |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Environment and Natural Resources Office (MENRO)          |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                  |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                               |
| **Who May Avail**             | Business establishments, institutions, communities, and individuals |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 27 minutes (plus payment time at MTO)                               |
| **Fees**                      | ₱365.00 / ₱730.00 / ₱1,095.00 (depending on collection frequency)   |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Inquire and submit application form

- Approach the MENRO front desk
- Inquire about garbage collection services
- Receive and complete the garbage collection application form

### Step 2: Attend ESWM orientation

- Attend Ecological Solid Waste Management (ESWM) orientation
- Learn waste segregation requirements and procedures under Republic Act 9003

### Step 3: Pay fees at the Municipal Treasurer's Office

- Proceed to the MTO and pay the garbage collection fee (₱365.00, ₱730.00, or ₱1,095.00)
- Obtain your Official Receipt

### Step 4: Return to MENRO and receive approval

- Return to MENRO with your Official Receipt
- MENRO issues and releases your approved garbage collection application and service schedule

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Business Permit _(for commercial, institutional, or industrial establishments)_ — from Business Permits and Licensing Office (BPLO)
- [ ] Official Receipt _(after payment at MTO)_
- [ ] Waste segregation compliance confirmation

### Eligibility

- All residents and property owners in the municipality
- Business establishments and institutions
- Communities and barangays

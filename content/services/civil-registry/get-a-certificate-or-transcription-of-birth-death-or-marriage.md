# Get a Certificate or Transcription of Birth, Death, or Marriage — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Civil Registry Office issues Certificates of Transcription for birth, death, and marriage records on file with the office. These are official copies of civil registry documents for use in legal, personal, or official transactions.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                         |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Civil Registry Office                                 |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                              |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                           |
| **Who May Avail**             | Document owner, parents, guardian, or authorized representative |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 13 minutes                                                      |
| **Fees**                      | Per Revenue Code (fee amount confirmed at the office)           |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Proceed to the Civil Registry Office and fill out a request slip

- Visit the Municipal Civil Registry Office and present your complete requirements.
- Get and fill out the request slip.
- Staff will verify, search for, and retrieve the document, then issue an Order of Payment (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Pay at the Municipal Treasurer's Office

- Proceed to the MTO and pay the applicable fee.
- An Official Receipt will be issued.

### Step 3: Return to the Civil Registry Office and receive your document

- Return and present your Official Receipt.
- Staff checks and records the OR (approximately 1 minute).
- The Municipal Civil Registrar signs and releases the Certificate of Transcription (approximately 2 minutes).

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID _(for the document owner or parents)_
- [ ] Authorization Letter or Special Power of Attorney (SPA) and Valid ID _(for authorized representatives)_

# Get Receipts for Police Clearance, Livestock Registration, and Other Fees — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Treasurer's Office issues Official Receipts for various municipal fees including police clearance fees, registration of large cattle, and other government-required payments. Bring your Order Slip or Tax Bill from the requesting office before proceeding to pay.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                        |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Treasurer's Office                   |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                             |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                          |
| **Who May Avail**             | All individuals with a pending fee payment     |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 5 minutes                                      |
| **Fees**                      | Varies depending on the service or transaction |

---

## How to Avail

### Step 1: Secure your Order Slip or Tax Bill from the requesting office

- Before going to the MTO, secure an Order Slip or Tax Bill from the office that requires the payment (e.g., PNP for police clearance, MAO for large cattle registration).

### Step 2: Proceed to the MTO and pay

- Present your Order Slip, Tax Bill, or other related documents to the Revenue Collection Clerk.
- The clerk issues your Official Receipt upon payment (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 3: Return to the requesting office with your Official Receipt

- Bring your Official Receipt back to the office that required the payment to complete your transaction.

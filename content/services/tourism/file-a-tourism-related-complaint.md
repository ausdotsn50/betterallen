# File a Tourism-Related Complaint — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Tourism Office (MTO) accepts and processes complaints related to tourism services and tourism establishments in Allen. If a resolution is not available within the MTO, the complaint is endorsed to the appropriate government offices or agencies.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                      |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Tourism Office (MTO)                               |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                           |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                        |
| **Who May Avail**             | Tourists and tourism-related establishments                  |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 10 minutes (initial review); full resolution within 5–7 days |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                         |

---

## How to File a Complaint

### Step 1: Fill out and submit the Complaint Form

- Get a Complaint Form from the MTO and fill it out completely.
- Submit the accomplished form to the Tourism Officer.
- Staff reviews the complaint and identifies available solutions (approximately 10 minutes).

### Step 2: MTO provides resolution or endorses the complaint

- If a solution is available at the MTO level, it will be provided within 3–5 days.
- If the complaint falls outside the MTO's jurisdiction, it will be endorsed to the appropriate offices or agencies within 1 day.

### Step 3: Receive an update on the status of your complaint

- The Tourism Officer informs and coordinates with you on the status or resolution of your complaint within 1 day of endorsement or resolution.

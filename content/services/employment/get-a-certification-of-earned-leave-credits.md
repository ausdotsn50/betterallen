# Get a Certification of Earned Leave Credits — Municipality of Allen

The Human Resource Management Office (HRMO) issues a Certification of Earned Leave Credits to incumbent officials and employees of the Local Government Unit of Allen who need an official document showing their current leave balance.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                            |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Human Resource Management Office (HRMO)            |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Government                           |
| **Who May Avail**             | Incumbent officials and employees of the LGU Allen |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 50 minutes                                         |
| **Fees**                      | None                                               |

---

## How to Request

### Step 1: Sign in the client logbook

- Sign in at the HRMO client logbook (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Fill out and submit the Requisition Form

- Receive and accomplish the Requisition Form provided by the HRMO (approximately 10 minutes).
- Submit the completed form to the HRMO staff.

### Step 3: Wait for processing

- Staff receives the accomplished form and begins processing your request (approximately 30 minutes total for retrieval and preparation).

### Step 4: Receive your Certification of Earned Leave Credits

- Staff issues the Certification of Earned Leave Credits to you (approximately 5 minutes).

---

## Requirements

### For the Principal / Concerned Individual

- [ ] Accomplished Requisition Form (1 original copy) — _Human Resource Management Office_
- [ ] Government-issued ID

### For a Representative

- [ ] Accomplished Requisition Form (1 original copy) — _Human Resource Management Office_
- [ ] Special Power of Attorney or Authorization Letter (1 original copy) — _Client_
- [ ] Government-issued ID of the person being represented (1 original + 1 photocopy)
- [ ] Government-issued ID of the Representative

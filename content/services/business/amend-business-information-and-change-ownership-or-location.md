# Amend Business Information and Change Ownership or Location — Municipality of Allen

If you are changing your business name, transferring ownership, or moving to a new business location, you must notify and update your records with the Business Permits and Licensing Section Office (BPLS). This keeps the Municipal Government's records current and prevents accumulation of taxes and penalties.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                        |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Business Permits and Licensing Section Office                  |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                             |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Business                                         |
| **Who May Avail**             | Business owners amending business name, ownership, or location |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 1 day (with complete requirements)                             |
| **Fees**                      | Applicable fees as assessed (if any)                           |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Secure the application form and submit your request letter and requirements

- Get the application form from the BPLS Office.
- Submit your request letter along with all required documents.
- BPLS staff will encode your details and assess any applicable fees.

### Step 2: Receive your Order of Payment

- BPLS prepares and signs your Order of Payment.
- You will receive the signed Order of Payment and your application letter.
- Proceed to the Municipal Treasurer's Office (MTO) to pay any fees due.

### Step 3: Pay required fees

- Pay at the MTO cashier and receive your Official Receipt.
- Return to the BPLS and present your Official Receipt.

### Step 4: Claim your updated records

- BPLS updates your record in the system.
- You will receive the updated application form reflecting your changes.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Request letter indicating the reason and date of the intended change (business name, ownership transfer, or location/address change)
- [ ] Surrendered old Business Permit
- [ ] Barangay-level business closure certificate _(close your old barangay registration first)_
- [ ] New Barangay Clearance from the new business address — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] New Business Permit application (Municipal level)
- [ ] Sketch plan or map of the new business location
- [ ] Board Resolution regarding the change _(for corporations and cooperatives only)_
- [ ] BIR transfer documentation _(if transferring from one Revenue District Office to another)_

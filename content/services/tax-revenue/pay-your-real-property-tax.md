# Pay Your Real Property Tax — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Treasurer's Office accepts Real Property Tax (RPT) payments for all landowners and property holders in Allen. Paying your real property tax on time avoids penalties and keeps your property records in good standing.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                     |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Treasurer's Office                |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                          |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                       |
| **Who May Avail**             | All real property owners in Allen           |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 5–7 minutes                                 |
| **Fees**                      | Based on the assessed value of the property |

---

## How to Pay

### Step 1: Proceed to the MTO and state your payment

- Approach the Revenue Collection Clerk and state that you are paying your real property tax.
- The clerk will ask for your name and the location of your property.

### Step 2: Property record lookup and computation

- The clerk looks up your Real Property Card and payment history.
- Your tax due is computed based on the assessed value of your property.

### Step 3: Pay and receive your Official Receipt

- Pay the computed amount.
- An Official Receipt is issued to you as proof of payment (approximately 5–7 minutes total).

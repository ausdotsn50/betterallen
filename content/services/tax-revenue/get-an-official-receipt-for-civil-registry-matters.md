# Get an Official Receipt for Civil Registry Matters — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Treasurer's Office issues Official Receipts for payments related to civil registry documents and transactions requested from the Local Civil Registry Office (LCRO). You must first request your document from the LCRO before proceeding to the MTO to pay.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                             |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Treasurer's Office                                        |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                  |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                               |
| **Who May Avail**             | All individuals with a pending civil registry transaction           |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 5 minutes                                                           |
| **Fees**                      | ₱50.00 (base fee; may vary depending on the civil registry service) |

---

## How to Avail

### Step 1: Request your document from the Local Civil Registry Office first

- Visit the LCRO and request the civil registry document or service you need.
- The LCRO will provide a pay slip or order of payment directing you to the MTO.

### Step 2: Proceed to the MTO and pay

- Present your pay slip or Order of Payment from the LCRO to the Revenue Collection Clerk.
- Pay the applicable fee.
- The Revenue Clerk issues your Official Receipt (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 3: Return to the LCRO with your Official Receipt

- Bring your Official Receipt back to the LCRO to complete the processing of your civil registry document.

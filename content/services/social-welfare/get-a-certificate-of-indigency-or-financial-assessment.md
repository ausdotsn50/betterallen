# Get a Certificate of Indigency or Financial Assessment — Municipality of Allen

The MSWDO issues Certificates of Indigency and Financial Assessment to residents who need official documentation of their economic status for accessing government assistance, medical programs, scholarships, or other social services.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                  |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office (MSWDO)  |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                       |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                    |
| **Who May Avail**             | Indigent residents and individuals in need of assistance |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Approximately 35 minutes                                 |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                     |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Approach the MSWDO and present your documents

- Visit the MSWDO and state your purpose for requesting the certificate.
- Staff records your details in the client logbook.
- Your documents are verified (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Undergo an intake interview and assessment

- The Social Worker conducts a brief interview and assessment of your situation (approximately 10–20 minutes).

### Step 3: Receive your certificate

- The certificate is prepared (approximately 10 minutes) and issued to you (approximately 5 minutes).

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Certificate of Indigency from the Barangay — _Office of the Punong Barangay_
- [ ] Valid ID — _any government-issued ID_

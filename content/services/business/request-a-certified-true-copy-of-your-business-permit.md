# Request a Certified True Copy of Your Business Permit — Municipality of Allen

The Business Permits and Licensing Section Office (BPLS) issues certified true copies of your Business Permit for official use, documentation, or compliance purposes.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                             |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Business Permits and Licensing Section Office       |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                  |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Business                              |
| **Who May Avail**             | Business permit holders requesting a certified copy |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 30 minutes                                          |
| **Fees**                      | Certification fee as indicated in the Revenue Code  |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your request letter and requirements

- Submit your letter of request together with the original Business Permit and 3 photocopies at the BPLS Office.
- Frontline personnel will check and receive your documents.

### Step 2: Receive your Order of Payment

- BPLS staff issues an Order of Payment for the certification fee.
- Proceed to the Municipal Treasurer's Office (MTO) cashier to pay.

### Step 3: Pay the certification fee

- Pay at the MTO cashier and receive your Official Receipt.

### Step 4: Present your receipt and claim the certified true copy

- Return to the BPLS and present your Official Receipt.
- Staff prepares and signs/approves the Certified True Copy of your Business Permit.
- Claim your certified document from the frontline personnel.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Letter of request
- [ ] Original Business Permit
- [ ] 3 photocopies of the original Business Permit
- [ ] Payment of the certification fee (as per Revenue Code)

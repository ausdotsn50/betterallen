# Get a Person with Disability (PWD) Identification Card — Municipality of Allen

The Person with Disability Affairs Office (PDAO) under the MSWDO issues official PWD ID cards to residents of Allen with disabilities. The PWD ID grants access to government privileges and benefits under the Magna Carta for Persons with Disability.

---

| Information                   | Details                                              |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Person with Disability Affairs Office (PDAO) / MSWDO |

| **Estimated Processing Time** | 5–10 minutes (within the same day)                   |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                 |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Fill up the Philippine Registry Form for Persons with Disability (PRFPWD)

- Get and accomplish the Philippine Registry Form for Persons with Disability at the PDAO/MSWDO office.
- Submit the filled-up form to the PDAO Focal Person for review.

### Step 2: Submit your documentary requirements

- Submit all required documents to the PDAO Focal Person.
- Staff will verify that you are not yet registered in the Philippine Registry for Persons with Disability.
- Your PWD ID card will be processed and checked for correctness.

### Step 3: Receive your PWD ID card

- Sign the logbook upon receipt of your PWD ID card.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Accomplished Philippine Registry Form for Persons with Disability (PRFPWD) — _PDAO/MSWDO_
- [ ] Certificate of Disability — _Public or Private Physician_
- [ ] Barangay Certificate of Residency or any government-issued ID — _Office of the Punong Barangay or any government office_

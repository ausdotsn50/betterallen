# Enroll in the Registry System for Basic Sector in Agriculture (RSBSA) — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Agriculture Office (MAO) facilitates enrollment in the Registry System for Basic Sector in Agriculture (RSBSA) and the National Coconut Farmers Registry System (NCFRS). Registration in these systems is required to qualify for government agricultural programs, subsidies, and benefits.

---

---

## How to Enroll

### Step 1: Go to the MAO and present your documents

- Visit the MAO and approach the staff.
- Staff will interview you and advise you on the documents you need to attach to your application (approximately 5 minutes).
- Staff assists you in filling out and signing the RSBSA form (approximately 10 minutes).

### Step 2: Secure the Barangay Captain's signature and return the form to the MAO

- Bring the accomplished form to your Barangay Captain for signature.
- Return the signed form to the MAO.
- MAO staff will check the documents and secure the signatures of the Municipal Agriculturist (MA) and the Municipal Agriculture and Fisheries Council (MAFC) Chairman (approximately 1 hour).

### Step 3: Submission to APCO

- MAO submits the completed RSBSA form with all attachments and signatures to the Agricultural Programs Coordinating Office (APCO) (approximately 1 day).

---

## Requirements

No specific checklist is required upfront — MAO staff will advise you on what documents to prepare during your visit. Commonly needed items include:

- Valid ID or proof of identity
- Proof of land ownership or tenancy (if applicable)
- Barangay Captain's signature on the RSBSA form

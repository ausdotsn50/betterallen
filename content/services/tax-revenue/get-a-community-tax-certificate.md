# Get a Community Tax Certificate — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Treasurer's Office issues Community Tax Certificates (CTC), commonly known as "cedula," to individuals and corporations as required for various government transactions and legal purposes.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                       |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Treasurer's Office  |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction            |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen         |
| **Who May Avail**             | All residents and individuals |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 5 minutes                     |
| **Fees**                      | ₱15.00                        |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Provide your personal information at the MTO

- Write your name, civil status, and birthdate on the required form.
- The Revenue Collection Clerk issues your Community Tax Certificate based on the given data (approximately 5 minutes).

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Old CTC _(if renewing)_ — _Municipal Treasurer's Office_
- [ ] Personal data or valid ID for reference

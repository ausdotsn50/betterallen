# Secure a Business Permit — Municipality of Allen

All business owners are required to secure a Business or Mayor's Permit before starting operations in Allen. Use the prescribed Unified Application Form and complete all requirements before the permit is issued.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                            |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Business Permits and Licensing Section Office      |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                 |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Business                             |
| **Who May Avail**             | All applicants for a new business permit           |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 2 days (with complete requirements)                |
| **Fees**                      | Based on applicable business taxes (paid annually) |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Secure and submit the Unified Application Form

- Get the Unified Application Form from the BPLS Office.
- Fill out the form completely and attach all required documents.
- Submit the accomplished form to the frontline personnel for verification.

### Step 2: Receive your Order of Payment

- The BPLS Officer-in-Charge will assess your application and prepare an Order of Payment.
- You will receive the signed Order of Payment and a copy of your application form.
- Proceed to the Municipal Treasurer's Office (MTO) to pay.

### Step 3: Pay fees, submit receipt, and claim your Business Permit

- Pay the required business tax fees at the MTO cashier.
- Receive your Official Receipt and return it to the BPLS receiving section.
- Affix your signature in the logbook and claim your Business Permit.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Proof of Registration (SEC for corporations, CDA for cooperatives, DTI for sole proprietorships)
- [ ] Proof of right to use business location:
  - If owned: Transfer Certificate of Title or Tax Declaration
  - If rented: Contract of Lease, Memorandum of Agreement, or written consent of property owner
- [ ] Location plan or sketch of business premises
- [ ] Locational Clearance — _Municipal Planning and Development Office_
- [ ] Fire Safety Inspection Certificate for Occupancy — _Bureau of Fire Protection_
- [ ] Occupancy Permit — _Building Official_
- [ ] Sanitary Permit or Sanitary Clearance — _Municipal Health Office_
- [ ] Barangay Clearance — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] Real Property Clearance — _Municipal Treasurer's Office_
- [ ] ECC or CNC Registration — _DENR_
- [ ] Municipal Solid Waste Compliance Certificate — _MENRO_
- [ ] Anti-Fencing Certificate _(for junkshops only)_ — _Philippine National Police_
- [ ] Certificate of Tax Exemption _(for hospitals, schools, gasoline stations, non-profits, cooperatives, dental clinics)_
- [ ] Other clearances, authorizations, or permits from national agencies as required by the line of business

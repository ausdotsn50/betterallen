# Submit a Job Application — Municipality of Allen

The Human Resource Management Office (HRMO) receives applications for vacant positions in the local government of Allen. All vacancies are open to the public, provided applicants meet the minimum qualifications required for the position. Vacancies are posted on the Civil Service Commission (CSC) website and at the HRMO bulletin board.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                          |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Office**                    | Human Resource Management Office (HRMO)          |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                               |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen / Government to Government |
| **Who May Avail**             | Any qualified applicant                          |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 30 minutes                                       |
| **Fees**                      | None                                             |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Get an Action Slip from the Mayor's Office and submit your application

- Secure an Action Slip from the Office of the Municipal Mayor.
- Submit your Letter of Application with all required attachments to the HRMO.
- The HRMO receives your application, endorses it internally, and reviews your documents to check if you qualify for the vacant position (approximately 25 minutes total).

### Step 2: Wait for the Notice of Screening

- If shortlisted, the HRMO will inform you and issue a Notice of Screening (approximately 5 minutes).

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Letter of Application addressed to the Municipal Mayor or Head of Agency — _Client_
- [ ] Action Slip — _Office of the Municipal Mayor_
- [ ] Updated Personal Data Sheet (CS Form 212) — _HRMO or CSC website_
- [ ] Transcript of Records and Diploma of highest educational attainment (1 photocopy) — _Client_
- [ ] Relevant Training Certificates (1 photocopy each) — _Client_
- [ ] Proof of Eligibility, if required (1 photocopy) — _Client_
- [ ] Other supporting documents as required — _Client_

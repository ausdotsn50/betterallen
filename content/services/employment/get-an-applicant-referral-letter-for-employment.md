# Get an Applicant Referral Letter for Employment — Municipality of Allen

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) issues referral letters for job applicants to prospective employers. Before the letter is issued, PESO conducts a pre-qualification process including job matching and career counseling to help ensure applicants are matched to suitable positions.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Approach the PESO Information Desk or check posted vacancies

- Visit the PESO Office and inquire about available job vacancies, or check posted job openings on the PESO Allen official Facebook page.
- Staff will refer you to the assigned focal person and issue a PESO Employment Information System (PEIS) Form and request slip (approximately 2 minutes).

### Step 2: Accomplish and submit your request slip and PEIS form

- Fill out the request slip and PEIS form.
- PESO staff checks your requirements and informs you of available job vacancies that match your qualifications (approximately 15 minutes).

### Step 3: Register in the logbook and receive your referral letter

- Sign the logbook.
- Staff prepares and issues your applicant referral letter addressed to the prospective employer (approximately 3 minutes).

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] 1 copy of accomplished Request Slip — _PESO Office_
- [ ] 1 copy of updated resume, biodata, or Curriculum Vitae — _Applicant_
- [ ] 1 copy each of Barangay Clearance, NBI Clearance, and Police Clearance — _Applicant_
- [ ] 1 copy of Transcript of Records or Diploma — _Applicant_
- [ ] 1 copy of accomplished National Skills Registration Program (NSRP) Form — _PESO Office_
- [ ] Other requirements as required by the company or establishment — _Applicant_

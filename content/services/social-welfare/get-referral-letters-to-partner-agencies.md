# Get Referral Letters to Partner Agencies — Municipality of Allen

The MSWDO issues referral letters to help residents access social services and assistance from other government agencies and partner organizations. These letters confirm your eligibility and situation to the receiving agency.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                 |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office (MSWDO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                      |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Government / Government to Citizen        |
| **Who May Avail**             | All citizens of Allen                                   |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | Approximately 48–68 minutes                             |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                    |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Visit the MSWDO and state your request

- Approach the MSWDO and explain which agency or service you need to be referred to.
- Staff records your details in the client logbook.
- Your documents are verified (approximately 10–20 minutes).

### Step 2: Intake interview

- The Social Worker conducts a brief interview (approximately 10–15 minutes).

### Step 3: Referral letter preparation and release

- The Social Worker prepares your referral letter (approximately 20–30 minutes).
- The completed referral letter is issued to you (approximately 3 minutes).

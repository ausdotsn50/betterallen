# Request Emergency Response Team Assistance — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (MDRRMO) deploys emergency response teams and rescuers during medical emergencies, vehicular accidents, near-drowning incidents, and other disasters. Call the MDRRMO hotline or go to the office immediately to request assistance.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                          |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (MDRRMO) |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                               |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Government                                         |
| **Who May Avail**             | All individuals, agencies, and organizations                     |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 12–40 minutes (from call to response team dispatch)              |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                             |

---

## How to Request

### Step 1: Call the MDRRMO hotline or go to the office in person

- Contact the MDRRMO immediately and provide all relevant details about the emergency.
- Staff will record all information from the caller (approximately 2–10 minutes) and organize the rescue team.

### Step 2: Wait for the response team at the incident site

- MDRRMO prepares the rescue vehicle and necessary equipment (approximately 30 minutes).
- The response team proceeds to your location.

---

## Information to Provide When Calling

Be ready to give the following details depending on the type of emergency:

**Medical Emergency**

- Your name (caller)
- Name of the patient (if known)
- Signs and symptoms currently experienced
- Past medical history (if known)

**Vehicular Accident**

- Your name (caller)
- Name of patient/s (if known)
- Exact location or site of the incident
- Type and number of vehicles involved
- Number of injured persons

**Near-Drowning Incident**

- Your name (caller)
- Exact location or site of the incident
- Number of persons involved

# Get an Electrical Permit — Municipality of Allen

The Municipal Engineering Office issues Electrical Permits for electrical installation and wiring work on residential and commercial properties in Allen. An Electrical Permit is required before any electrical work is carried out on a structure.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                                                                                             |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Municipal Engineering Office (MEO)                                                                                                  |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                                                                                  |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen                                                                                                               |
| **Who May Avail**             | All applicants for an Electrical Permit                                                                                             |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 19 minutes (with complete requirements)                                                                                             |
| **Fees**                      | Locational Clearance fee (based on Bill of Materials and structure) + Electrical fee (based on electrical fixtures and accessories) |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Submit your requirements to the MEO

- Visit the MEO and submit all required documents to the concerned personnel.
- Staff will check completeness and immediately inform you of any missing requirements (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Request a Locational Clearance from the MPDO

- MEO staff will prepare a request letter for an approved Locational Clearance from the Municipal Planning and Development Office (MPDO) (approximately 5 minutes).
- Locational Clearance fees apply, based on the Bill of Materials and structure type.

### Step 3: Submit approved Locational Clearance and request fee computation

- Submit your approved Locational Clearance to the MEO.
- Staff will prepare the computation of Electrical fees (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 4: Pay fees at the Municipal Treasurer's Office (MTO)

- Proceed to the MTO to pay the computed Electrical fee.
- Fees are based on electrical fixtures and accessories.
- The MTO will issue an Official Receipt.

### Step 5: Submit the Official Receipt and wait for Building Official's approval

- Return to the MEO and submit your Official Receipt.
- The Building Official will sign and approve the Electrical Permit (approximately 2 minutes).

### Step 6: Receive your approved Electrical Permit

- MEO staff records the transaction and releases your approved Electrical Permit and other associated documents (approximately 2 minutes).

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Electrical Application Permit Form
- [ ] Certificate of Final Electric Inspection or Certificate of Completion
- [ ] Electrical Load Schedule Form
- [ ] As-built plan or Authorized Barangay Electrician house wiring report — _MEO or Licensed Electrical Engineer_
- [ ] Barangay Certification — _Barangay Hall_
- [ ] Tax Declaration — _Municipal Treasurer's Office_
- [ ] Affidavit of Consent or Deed of Sale
- [ ] Vicinity Map — _Municipal Engineering Office_
- [ ] Site Development Plan — _Municipal Engineering Office_
- [ ] Bill of Materials — _Municipal Engineering Office_
- [ ] Approved Locational Clearance — _Municipal Planning and Development Office (MPDC)_

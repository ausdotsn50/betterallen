# Request Employment Verification — Municipality of Allen

The Human Resource Management Office (HRMO) accepts employment verification requests from financial institutions, other employers, or private individuals inquiring about the employment status of current or previous personnel of the Local Government Unit of Allen. Confidential information is only released with proper authorization.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information                   | Details                                                                           |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Office**                    | Human Resource Management Office (HRMO)                                           |
| **Classification**            | Simple Transaction                                                                |
| **Type of Transaction**       | Government to Citizen / Government to Government                                  |
| **Who May Avail**             | Financial institutions, employers, or private individuals requesting verification |
| **Estimated Processing Time** | 25 minutes                                                                        |
| **Fees**                      | None                                                                              |

---

## How to Request

### Step 1: Submit your requirements to the HRMO

- Present your valid ID and Authorization to Access Records to the HRMO staff.
- Staff receives and verifies submitted requirements (approximately 5 minutes).
- Staff pulls out the employee's 201 file (approximately 5 minutes).

### Step 2: Receive the verified employment information

- Staff provides the requested employment verification (approximately 10 minutes).
- **Note:** Confidential information will not be released without a copy of the Authorization to Access Employee Records.
- Staff records the date, time, information released, and details of the receiving party (approximately 5 minutes).
